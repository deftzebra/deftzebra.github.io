Interlude


I had always dreamed of going to North America for a vacation. The USA seems so luxurious, after all — L.A., Las Vegas, Malibu, Miami, New York City, Chicago… there are so many iconic places. I thought, maybe during university I’d get to go. Canada was never on my radar though, but… there we were, I guess. I have learned one thing, though. I hate flying. Why would anyone want to be more powerless?
