Player Settings

The player chooses the following:

Name

Text Entry

Avatar

The player can either select an avatar from a range of pre-made options or customize their avatar's face, body type, and outfit.

Narrator Voice

The player can listen to samples and select one of the following options: Soprano, Alto, Tenor, or Baritone.

Pronouns

The player can select from the following options: They/Them, She/They, He/They, She/Her, He/Him, or Ze/Hir

Accessibility

The player can customize features such as: Alt-text for images and videos, Text narration options, Ability to remap controls, and Choice of font size and style (Serif, Sans-serif, OpenDyslexic).
