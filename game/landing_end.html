Thank you for playing the game!

Take a moment to reflect on:

  • What interactions in the game led to a feeling of calm and peace of mind?
  • In which situations did you feel more resilient? What did you do or think that led to that feeling?
  • What do you need to do in future to maintain your peace of mind and resilience?

There are several activities that you could do to contemplate over the questions, such as journaling, mindfulness practice, writing a letter of support to a Ukrainian family or volunteering.

If you wish to support Ukraine, here are some ideas:

  • help a Ukrainian family locally where you live (ex., housing, job, education)
  • donate to the office of the President of Ukraine for the humanitarian aid
  • contribute to the purchase of equipment for the Ukrainian military with the Ukrainian NGO "Come Back Alive"
  • donate to the National Bank of Ukraine to raise funds for humanitarian aid or the Armed Forces of Ukraine
