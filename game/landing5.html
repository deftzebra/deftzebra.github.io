CLASSROOM, THE SOUNDS OF TALKING IN THE BACKGROUND

Welcome! Htaynga afh shpuy ttosoþn oen me þoþides.

The first day of class was terrifying. All they understood were hello and welcome, but multiple adults took them from room to room. The staff seemed to know why they were in Canada, but no one seemed to know what to say to make them feel better about transferring in. A sheet of paper was pressed into their hand, a schedule printed on it with vaguely-familiar subjects. The first one of the day, however, was English, and it was nothing like anything they had ever seen back home

Sedros dèlsûñ fmera esietoo ïætesyn di eaßl þaþð o lny mdt iwanne.
They speak so quickly… I think they said that class will start soon. I can walk around until then.
